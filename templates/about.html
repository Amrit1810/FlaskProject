Definition of Each Variable: Below Table shows the definition of each variable.

Data Values
primaryKey Unique identifier for each record.
name Full name of the individual.
age Age of the individual.
gender Gender of the individual.
linkedinProfURL LinkedIn profile URL of the individual.
designation Job title or designation of the individual.
country Country of residence of the individual.
disabled Boolean indicating whether the individual has a disability.
FreshExp Freshness of experience in the field.
joinedDate Date when the individual joined the organization.
profileDescription Brief description of the individual's professional profile.
username Username used by the individual.
email Email address of the individual.
password Password associated with the individual's account.
